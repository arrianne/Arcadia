About us

We deliver clear, practical building reports for strata committees, property managers and owners — so you can make confident decisions about your property.

Our story

Clear reports, trusted advice, practical outcomes.

Arcadia Building Reports was established to provide strata bodies, property managers and owners with straightforward building inspection services. We saw a need for reports that committees could understand and act on — without jargon, with clear photos, and with practical recommendations.

Our inspectors are licensed, insured and experienced in residential and commercial buildings across South East Queensland. We focus on condition assessment, defect documentation, and compliance-related observations to support your maintenance and capital planning.

Why choose us

  • Licensed and insured
  • Plain-language reports
  • Committee-friendly format
  • Responsive communication
