About us

We deliver clear, practical building reports for strata committees, property managers and owners — so you can make confident decisions about your property.

Our story

Clear reports, trusted advice, practical outcomes.

A full-service building inspections company that provides services to the Strata and Real Estate industries. Our first priority is to provide an unsurpassed level of quality and assurance in terms of the product we produce as it is critical to the proper maintenance and insurance risks associated with the major assets that we inspect.

We are fully licenced. Unlike many of our competitors we hold a building inspection licence with the QBCC and we are also licenced to carry out pool inspections.

Michael Conway

Director, General Manager and Owner of Arcadia

Michael has held an open builders licence with the QBCC since 2007 and has worked in the construction industry for over 25 years. Michael's experience includes over 15 years in commercial construction with one of Queensland's former major building companies and over 10 years in residential construction running his own building company. Michael also has experience in building inspections in regards to property maintenance, defects and work place health and safety.

Throughout his years in the building industry Michael has developed skills that he believes are crucial in delivering positive outcomes and building relationships. His first priority is always the quality of the product he produces to ensure his client's needs are met.

Steve Drew

Director, Operations Manager and Owner of Arcadia

Steve has held a Cert IV in Carpentry since 1994 and has worked in the construction industry for over 25 years. Steve has a very hands-on working knowledge of construction and various building techniques. His eye for detail is his greatest strength and is a huge asset for his clients.
