Report types

Common report requests. Additional inspections available on request.

Maintenance condition reports

Building condition overview including defects, risks, priority items, and clear recommendations. Ideal for body corporates and committees planning maintenance works.

Roof inspections

Assessment of roof condition, drainage, and likely water ingress risks, supported with photos. Useful after leaks, storms, or for preventative planning.

Structural condition assessments

Practical review of structural concerns and visible indicators (movement/cracking). Designed to support decision-making and next steps.

Defect inspections

Identify defects and issues, document findings, and provide a clear summary of recommended actions. Suitable for new or existing buildings.

Dilapidation reports

Record existing conditions prior to nearby construction, tenancies, or works. Includes photo documentation to support future comparisons.

Safety assessments

Common-area safety checks and practical risk notes to help committees and managers prioritise action.

Balcony / barrier condition checks

Condition checks and observations relevant to balcony elements and barriers, with photographic evidence and practical recommendations.

Pre-purchase / due diligence style inspections

Practical building inspection for buyers and investors wanting a clear view of condition and likely issues.

Sinking fund support (report inputs)

Condition observations and priority recommendations to support long-term planning and capital works discussions.
