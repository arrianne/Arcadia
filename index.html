Our process

Simple process,
clear communication.

  1. 01

    Contact us

    Tell us what you need inspected. We’ll discuss your situation and recommend the right approach.

  2. 02

    Site inspection

    We attend site and document findings with photos. You’ll know when we’re coming and what to expect.

  3. 03

    Written report

    You receive a clear report with photos and recommendations, in plain language you can share with committees and managers.

  4. 04

    Next steps

    Optional follow-up to discuss priorities, timelines, or clarify any findings.
