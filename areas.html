South East Queensland

Areas we service

We provide building inspection services across Brisbane and South East Queensland — from the Gold Coast to the Sunshine Coast, and inland to Toowoomba.

Coverage area

Licensed to inspect buildings across Queensland. Most of our work is in South East Queensland, with Brisbane at the centre of our operations.

Brisbane Gold Coast Sunshine Coast Redlands Logan Moreton Bay Ipswich Toowoomba

Area

Brisbane

We're based in Brisbane and regularly inspect strata schemes, apartment buildings and commercial properties across the greater Brisbane area. This includes inner suburbs, Bayside, northern and southern corridors. Common requests include safety audits, maintenance reports, roof reports, sinking fund forecasting, and insurance valuations — with clear written reports and photos that committees and managers can act on.

Includes:

  • Inner Brisbane
  • Northern suburbs
  • Southern suburbs
  • Bayside

Area

Gold Coast

We service the Gold Coast and surrounding areas for building inspections, including Surfers Paradise, Broadbeach, Southport, Coomera and the hinterland. High-rise strata, townhouse complexes and resort-style developments are familiar territory. Coastal and high-rise buildings often have specific maintenance considerations — we're experienced in identifying water ingress, balcony and facade issues, and common-area safety.

Includes:

  • Surfers Paradise
  • Broadbeach
  • Southport
  • Northern Gold Coast

Area

Sunshine Coast

We travel to the Sunshine Coast for building reports in Mooloolaba, Maroochydore, Caloundra, Noosa and the broader region. Strata committees and property managers regularly engage us for condition and compliance-focused reporting. Whether it's a beachside complex or hinterland strata scheme, we provide clear documentation to support maintenance planning, risk management, and capital works decisions.

Includes:

  • Mooloolaba
  • Maroochydore
  • Caloundra
  • Noosa

Area

Redlands, Logan, Moreton Bay & Ipswich

We service the broader South East Queensland region including Redlands Bay, Logan, Moreton Bay (Caboolture, Redcliffe, North Lakes and surrounds) and Ipswich. Many of these areas have growing strata populations and established commercial buildings. For properties further afield, we can discuss travel and scheduling — contact us with your location and requirements for a tailored quote.

Also includes:

  • Toowoomba (on request)
  • Wide Bay (on request)
